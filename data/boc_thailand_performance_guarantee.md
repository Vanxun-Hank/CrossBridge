# Performance Guarantee

Performance Guarantee

Name

Performance Guarantee

Introduction

It is a written document issued by Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited at the request of the customer to the owner of a contracted project or the buyer of a sales deal, guaranteeing that the applicant will strictly perform the contracts.

Functions

Guarantee proper fulfillment of contractual obligations and the quality of the subject matter of contract; solve the problem of mutual mistrust between the parties, and reduce capital occupation.

Features

1. Solve the problem of mutual mistrust between both parties of a transaction.With good reputation, Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited involves in the transaction and acts as a guarantor to ensure the smooth transaction by providing guarantee for the parties.

2. Ensure the fulfillment of contractual obligations. Ensure the fulfillment of contractual obligations, and the quality of the subject matter under the contract.

3. Indemnify the damaged party and penalize the defaulting party.Indemnify the damaged party and penalize the defaulting party in case of breach of contract to avoid and reduce the contract breaching activities, thus saving the time and expenses arising from litigation or arbitration.

Charges

All charges are subject to the standard charge rates for international settlement released by Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited.

Target Customers

1. Contractors in the engineering projects;

2. Suppliers in the material procurement projects.

Application Qualifications

1. The applicant shall hold a business license of a legal person that has passed the annual inspection or other valid certificates which can fully prove its operation and scope of business.

2. The applicant shall have the qualification to engage in the relevant business (such as the qualification of contracting international projects, construction qualification of building enterprises, etc.).

3. The applicant shall have a credit line at Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited, or pay full margin, or full guarantee acceptable to Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited.

Procedure

I. Application for letter of guarantee

1. The applicant fills out an application form.

2. The applicant pays the margin or has the relevant credit line.

3. The applicant submits primary documents related to the contract.

4. The applicant submits other documents necessary for the issuance of a letter of guarantee as required by Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited.

II. Verification and issuance of letter of guarantee

1. Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited verifies the customer's qualification, underlying transaction and relevant documents.

2. Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited confirms the customer's margin or credit line.

3. Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited verifies the written application and the form of letter of guarantee.

4. Bank of China (Thai) Public Company Limited issues the letter of guarantee.

Tips

1. Amendment to letter of guarantee: On the request of the guaranteed party and the beneficiary, the clauses under the letter of guarantee can be amended, for example, the validity period can be extended.

2. Payment/compensation against letter of guarantee: If the beneficiary claims compensation within the validity period of the guarantee, the applicant shall be notified in time. The payment shall be made after the guarantee issuing bank verifies the claim documents and confirms that they are in compliance with the claim clauses under the guarantee.
