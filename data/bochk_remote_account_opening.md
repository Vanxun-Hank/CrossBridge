Customers can choose "Remote Account Opening Service" 1 , enjoy account opening online without visiting our branches.

Remarks:

Basic application requirements for "Remote Account Opening Service":(i) The Applicant should be Hong Kong Incorporated Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, Limited Company (including Limited by Guarantee Company) with annual sales turnover which meets our designated conditions; (ii) The Applicant should not has Corporate Shareholder; and (iii) Related Party(ies) of the Applicant should not exceed 10 and shall be a Hong Kong Identity Card holder(s).

The Applicant may choose to use "Remote Account Opening Service" when the above basic application requirements and the designated conditions of our bank are fulfilled. (Remarks: Even if "Remote Account Opening Service" is being preliminarily chosen, subject to the actual circumstance or the further information provided, we may alternately require visit to our bank for completing the account opening procedure.)

Opening a business account for sole proprietorship, partnership, and single-layer limited companies with share capital that are registered in Hong Kong can be completed earliest in 3 working days counting from completing the application submission, submit the required documents and pay the account opening fee.
