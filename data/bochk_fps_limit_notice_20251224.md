# Notice on Adjustment of the Schedule of General Banking Service Charges

Bank of China (Hong Kong) Limited issued this customer notice on 24 December
2025.

The notice states that, with effect from 26 January 2026 unless otherwise
specified, BOCHK will revise specified service charge items while other items
remain unchanged.

## Local Bank Transfer via FPS

BOCHK revises the transaction limit for Local Bank Transfer via FPS.

Service item:

- Local Bank Transfer
- FPS (Faster Payment System)
- Funds transfer to other local banks through FPS

Revised transfer amount:

- Transfer amount is equal to or not more than HKD3,000,000.00 or equivalent.

Fees:

- Personal Customers: Waived.

Revision detail:

- The transaction limit will be increased from "equal to or not more than
  HKD1,000,000.00" to "equal to or not more than HKD3,000,000.00".

## Related Remittance Service Items

The same notice also revises remittance-related service items.

For Outward Remittance, Telegraphic Transfer / Global Remittance:

- Conducted via branches: HKD260.00 per item.
- Conducted via electronic channels:
  - Personal customers: HKD65.00 per item.
  - Corporate customers: HKD120.00 per item.

## Enquiry

For enquiry, the notice directs customers to contact BOCHK staff or call:

- Personal Customer Service Hotline: (852) 3988 2388.
- Corporate Customer Service Hotline: (852) 3988 2288.

The notice says customers can download it from "What's New" in the BOCHK website
on or before 26 February 2026 and may not be able to access or download it
afterwards. It also states that, should there be any discrepancy between the
English and Chinese versions, the Chinese version shall prevail.
